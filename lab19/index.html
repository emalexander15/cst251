Lab Nineteen: CSS Framework




Purpose


The purpose of this lab was to begin experimenting with Bootstrap's navbar and grid layout. In lab 17 and 18 we started to become familiar with different layout techniques as well as layout methods for a multitude of device screens. As usual we also worked with partners to expand our knowledge, help one another out, and teach each other.




Challenges


The challenges I encountered during this lab involved the navbar as well as borders with the grid layout. I was unable to figure out how to customize a color scheme with the navbar (I ended up making it transparent instead) after looking on both w3schools and Bootstrap. I also dislike the borders being connected in the column content as this created a thicker line that seems unbalancing to the rest of the aesthetic. Similar to Monica, this was also a very new and complicated concept to grasp and the lecture slides were not very descriptive or visual in doing so, so we resorted to w3schools.
