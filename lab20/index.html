Lab twenty: team project sketches



Purpose

The purpose of this lab was to create a series of prototype designs for our website topic to later show our team members and recieve feedback. The sketches were to be done quickly and we will later narrow down our ideas to three final detailed prototypes.



Challenges

The challenges I encountered were thinking of different design concepts for the same topic. Our playlist idea is pretty straight forward, but it was hard to think of different design concepts as there is only so many ways you can have a model of a playlist like Spotify or Apple Music.



Results

Look below for my prototypes!

I was successfuly able to create 15 sketches of 5 different prototype ideas. I drew concepts for the title page having statistics or links to our playlists as well as a nav bar which directs the viewer to our playlists. I also came up with ideas for our individual subtopic pages and how to layout the playlist.
